Description: It's been humorously claimed that excessive bread consumption may lead to hearing imaginary baguette orchestras playing symphonies in your head.

Don't panic! Before you start avoiding bread altogether, we should let you in on the secret. These "reasons" are entirely fabricated and intended for amusement, not to be taken seriously. In reality, bread is a safe and nutritious food source.
